Welcome to the Farm

Located in Pap-Onditi, Nyakach, Sally Anne's Farm is a progressive enterprise dedicated to organic excellence and sustainable rural development.

Our Livestock

Dairy Goats: Producing fresh milk, creamy yoghurt, and natural goat milk soap.

Poultry Farming: Healthy, farm-raised chickens for the community.

Rabbit Farming: Utilizing rabbit urine as high-quality natural fertilizer.

Aquaculture

  • Fish pond farming: Sustainable rearing of Tilapia and Catfish.
  • Sun-dried fish: Processed for wider access and longer shelf life.
  • Nutrition awareness: Empowering the community through healthy protein sources.

Fresh & Dried Vegetables

Nutritious, affordable, and sustainable produce grown with organic care.

  • Green leafy vegetables: Farm-fresh daily produce.
  • Sun-dried vegetables: Available for customers with limited access to fresh greens.

Value Addition

  • Goat milk yoghurt: Creamy and nutritious.
  • Goat milk soap: Natural skincare solution.
  • Sun-dried fish: Preserved for convenience and nutrition.

Our Commitment

  • Organic and eco-friendly farming practices.
  • Sustainable rural development in Nyakach.
  • Community empowerment through agriculture.
